I ♥ Color

Color really isn't that difficult to get it right, if you know where to start.

Learn by doing and you'll be a master in no time.

  • Blue

    Blue is the color of the sky and the sea. It is often associated with depth and stability. It symbolizes trust, loyalty, wisdom, confidence, intelligence, faith, truth, and heaven.

  • Green

    Green is the color of the nature. It symbolizes growth, harmony, freshness, and fertility. Green has a strong emotional correspodence with safety.

  • Red

    Red is the color of fire and blood, so it is associated with energy, war, danger, strength, power, determination as well as passion, desire, and love.
